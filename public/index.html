About Us

China Bowl is a family owned Chinese food restaurant located in Langley, BC. We specialize in Cantonese and Sezchuan style cuisine. Our services include dine-in, take-out, and free delivery within 5km and a minimum order of $25. In addition, we also offer catering for large scaled events (contact us for more details).
